Review a client-facing deliverable for readiness, professionalism, and West Monroe engagement standards.

Review the referenced deliverable, or the most recent deliverable in context, against West Monroe client engagement standards.

This review is for pre-delivery quality assurance. The goal is to catch issues before the client sees the work.

Perform a structured review across these dimensions. For each dimension, list specific issues with location references. If a dimension has no issues, say `No issues found.`

## 1. Accuracy & Completeness

Check for:

- unsupported claims or data points
- visible TODO, TBD, or placeholder text
- missing sections expected for this type of deliverable
- conclusions that are not supported by the analysis

## 2. Client Data Handling

Check for:

- inappropriate client confidential information
- data from one client appearing in a deliverable for another
- overly specific data points that create identification risk
- missing classification markings if required

## 3. Tone & Professionalism

Check for:

- informal language that is inappropriate for client delivery
- internal shorthand, jargon, or team nicknames visible to the client
- hedging language that undermines credibility
- recommendations without justification
- framing that sounds critical of the client without being constructive

## 4. Technical Accuracy

Check for:

- technical claims that conflict with established facts
- architecture or design recommendations with obvious unacknowledged risks
- code samples that would not work as written
- outdated version or compatibility claims

## 5. Actionability

Check for:

- recommendations without next steps or owners
- estimates without assumptions
- open questions that should be resolved before delivery
- findings without business impact framing

## 6. Formatting & Polish

Check for:

- inconsistent heading hierarchy
- missing tables or diagrams that are referenced
- broken links or references
- inconsistent capitalization or terminology

After the review, provide a **Delivery Readiness Summary**:

- `Ready to deliver` / `Ready with minor edits` / `Needs revision before delivery`
- top 3 issues to address before sending to the client
- any sections that are especially strong
